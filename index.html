Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions from international pharmaceutical buyers, importers, and distributors.

What is the minimum order quantity for pharmaceutical exports?
Minimum order quantities vary by product type. Most formulations start from 100 units for initial trial orders. Bulk and repeat orders attract better pricing. Contact us with your specific product and quantity requirement for a detailed quote.
Which countries does Freed Pharma LLP export to?
We export to 50+ countries including USA, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Kenya, Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, Uganda, Tanzania, Nepal, Sweden, Germany, and many more. Contact us to confirm availability for your specific country.
Can you supply Semaglutide and Tirzepatide from India?
Yes. Freed Pharma LLP is a verified Semaglutide and Tirzepatide supplier from India. We source from WHO-GMP certified manufacturers and provide full documentation including Certificate of Analysis (COA) and Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) with every shipment.
What export documents do you provide?
We provide Certificate of Analysis (COA), Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS), commercial invoice, packing list, and certificate of origin. We can also assist with regulatory dossiers and import permit guidance based on your destination country's requirements.
How quickly do you respond to purchase inquiries?
We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent or time-sensitive requirements, contact us directly via WhatsApp at +91 7977006670 for immediate response. Our export team is available Monday–Saturday.
Are you a licensed pharmaceutical exporter from India?
Yes. Freed Pharma LLP holds IEC (Import Export Code: AAIFF2262P), GSTIN (27AAIFF2262P1Z0), is registered as an LLP (LLPIN: ABA-4164), and is a Udyam-registered MSME (UDYAM-MH-19-0123574). All our exports are fully legal, documented, and compliant.
Do you supply to hospitals and healthcare institutions?
Yes. We supply to hospitals, clinics, healthcare institutions, pharmaceutical distributors, importers, research organizations, and pharmacies. We handle both regular wholesale orders and institutional procurement requests with appropriate documentation.
What shipping methods are available for pharmaceutical exports?
We ship via air freight, sea freight, and express courier (DHL, FedEx, EMS) depending on the product type, quantity, and urgency. Cold-chain logistics can be arranged for temperature-sensitive products like peptides and injectables.
